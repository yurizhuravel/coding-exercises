Which path to choose

Basically, you have three options:

Front end

Front-end guys deal with what users see and experience. They make websites great-looking, user-friendly and interactive. They also generally have some understaniding of usability and design basics.

Back end

These are code ninjas who deal with the dark side: the servers and the way they process and store data. If your website looks cool but works slowly and unreliably, that's usualy a back end developer's fault.

Full stack

You may think of these guys as of Swiss knife coders who can both create user interfaces and deal with servers. They are especially useful in startups and small companies which can't afford coder specialisation.
