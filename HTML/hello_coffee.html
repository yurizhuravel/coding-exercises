Hello Coffee!

A few facts about the mankind's favourite brew



Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. Once ripe, coffee beans are picked, processed, and dried. Green (unroasted) coffee beans are one of the most traded agricultural commodities in the world. Once traded, the coffee beans are roasted to varying degrees, depending on the desired flavor. Roasted beans are ground and brewed to produce coffee as a beverage.

About coffee

  • The Coffea plant is native to subtropical Africa and some islands in southern Asia
  • Coffee plants are now cultivated in over 70 countries
  • The two most commonly grown plants are arabica and robusta
  • Coffee cultivation first took place in Abyssinia
  • The earliest credible evidence of coffee-drinking appears in the middle of the 15th century

Three common brewing methods

  1. Boiling
  2. Steeping
  3. Pressurising
